What are ICOs?

Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) are a fundraising method where new cryptocurrency projects sell their tokens to early investors, similar to an Initial Public Offering (IPO) in the stock market.

What are Airdrops?

Airdrops are distributions of free tokens from cryptocurrency projects to wallet addresses. Projects use airdrops to create awareness, reward users, and distribute tokens more widely.

Why Participate?

  • Early access to innovative blockchain projects
  • Potential for significant returns if the project succeeds
  • Opportunity to support the development of new technologies
  • Free tokens through participation in airdrops
Risk Warning: ICOs and new token investments carry significant risk. Always conduct your own research (DYOR) and never invest more than you can afford to lose.
